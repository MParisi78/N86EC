## Airspeeds (V-Speeds)

*All speeds are Calibrated Airspeed (CAS). This POH is in MPH; KTS shown for cross-reference.*

| Speed | Meaning | MPH | KTS |
|---|---|---:|---:|
| **Vso** | Stall, landing config (flaps down) | **55** | 48 |
| **Vs** | Stall, clean (flaps up) | **64** | 56 |
| **Vx** | Best angle of climb | **74** | 64 |
| **Vy** | Best rate of climb | **85** | 74 |
| **Vglide** | Best glide | **80** | 70 |
| **Vfe** | Max flap extended | **115** | 100 |
| **Va** | Design maneuvering (at 2150 lb gross) | **129** | 112 |
| **Vno** | Max structural cruise (top of green arc) | **140** | 122 |
| **Vne** | Never exceed | **171** | 149 |
| Flap operating range | | 55–115 | 48–100 |

**Va at reduced weight** decreases with weight. At 1800 lb, use ~119 MPH. Do a proportional check against the source table on **[page 51](pages/page-051.jpg)** for your actual weight.

## Stall speeds by bank angle (MPH CAS, power off)

| Bank | 0° | 20° | 40° | 50° | 60° |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| **Flaps up** | 64 | 66 | 73 | 80 | 91 |
| **Flaps down** | 55 | 57 | 63 | 69 | 78 |

Bank angle costs you speed margin fast. At 60° bank clean, you stall at Vy.

## Emergency memory items

### ⚠ Engine failure on takeoff (with runway remaining)

1. Throttle — **CLOSED**
2. Land straight ahead
3. Brakes as required

### ⚠ Engine failure on takeoff (no runway remaining)

1. Maintain **safe airspeed** (do NOT stall trying to turn back)
2. Shallow turns only, to avoid obstacles
3. Fuel selector — **OFF**
4. Mixture — **IDLE CUT-OFF**
5. Master switch — **OFF**
6. Land straight ahead / minor course changes only

### ⚠ Engine failure in flight (attempt restart)

1. Airspeed — **~80 MPH (best glide)**
2. Fuel selector — **switch tanks**
3. Fuel boost pump — **ON**
4. Mixture — **RICH**
5. Carburetor heat — **ON**
6. Magnetos — check **L**, **R**, then **BOTH**
7. Primer — check **LOCKED**

### ⚠ Engine failure — no restart, forced landing

1. Airspeed — **80 MPH (best glide)**
2. Field selected, head into wind if possible
3. Fuel selector — **OFF**
4. Mixture — **IDLE CUT-OFF**
5. Magnetos — **OFF**
6. Master switch — **OFF (after flaps set)**
7. Seat belts & shoulder harness — **TIGHT**
8. Cabin door — **UNLATCHED before touchdown**
9. Flaps — as required
10. Touchdown at **minimum controllable airspeed**

### ⚠ Fire on the ground (start)

1. Mixture — **IDLE CUT-OFF**
2. Throttle — **OPEN**
3. Continue cranking to draw fire through carburetor
4. If fire persists: Master **OFF**, evacuate

### ⚠ Fire in flight

1. Source: **electrical** → Master **OFF**, land as soon as practicable
2. Source: **engine** → Fuel selector **OFF**, mixture **IDLE CUT-OFF**, throttle **CLOSED**, cabin heat/vents **OFF**, forced landing per procedure

### ⚠ Loss of oil pressure

1. Land at nearest suitable airport as soon as possible
2. Anticipate power loss / seizure — be ready for forced landing

### ⚠ Alternator failure

1. Reduce electrical load immediately
2. Verify with ammeter
3. Master switch cycle (OFF then ON) — attempt reset
4. If unable to restore: land as soon as practical to preserve battery

## Normal procedure highlights

### Preflight quick items
- Master **ON** → check fuel gauges → Master **OFF**
- Fuel: **visual check + drain sumps** (both tanks + gascolator)
- Oil: **8 qt max**, dipstick seated
- Control surfaces: **free and correct**
- Prop & spinner: **defects, nicks**

### Before start
- Parking brake **ON**
- Carb heat **COLD (OFF)**
- Fuel selector **desired tank**
- Mixture **IDLE CUT-OFF** until engine fires

### Starting (cold)
1. Master **ON**, boost pump **ON** (3 sec), pump **OFF**
2. Mixture **RICH**, throttle **cracked**
3. Magneto to **START**
4. When engine catches → throttle to **800 RPM**
5. Oil pressure within **30 sec** or shut down

### Run-up (at 2000 RPM)
- Magnetos: max drop **175 RPM**, max differential **50 RPM**
- Carb heat: check for **RPM drop** (confirms hot air)
- Alternator: check charge
- Vacuum: check in green
- Idle: **600–700 RPM**, mixture check

### Takeoff
- Full throttle smoothly
- Rotate **~65 MPH**
- Climb at **85 MPH (Vy)** to pattern altitude; use **74 MPH (Vx)** if obstacle

### Cruise
- Lean above 5,000 ft or 75% power for smooth operation
- Monitor: fuel gauges, oil temp/press, fuel press

### Descent / approach
- Carb heat **ON** any time throttle is closed below cruise power
- Pattern: **90 MPH downwind**, **80 MPH base**, **75 MPH final**
- Full flaps by short final
- Over the fence: **~70 MPH**

### After landing / shutdown
- Flaps **UP**, carb heat **COLD**
- Throttle idle 1000 RPM to cool
- Mixture **IDLE CUT-OFF** to stop
- Magnetos **OFF**, master **OFF**

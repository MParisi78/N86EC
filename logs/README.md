# N86EC Aircraft Logs (Text Transcriptions)

Piper PA-28-140 Cherokee, S/N 28-7525026, registration N86EC (originally N105MS). These are plain-text/Markdown transcriptions of the scanned logbook PDFs in the repository root, produced so the handwritten and typed entries are searchable and easy to read without opening a PDF.

Transcriptions are best-effort: original entries are handwritten, and some words, dates, or certificate numbers are noted as illegible or uncertain where the scan quality made them impossible to read with confidence. Refer back to the source PDFs for anything safety- or compliance-critical.

## Chronology

The two airframe logs form one continuous record: Book 1 runs 1974–2005 and hands off directly into Book 2, which continues 2006–2026 and documents the N105MS → N86EC registration change.

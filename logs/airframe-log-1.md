# Airframe Log (Book 1) — N86EC (originally N105MS)

Transcribed from `render_pdf.pdf`, the original Piper Aircraft Corporation "Aircraft Log" (Part No. 230715, General Offices at Lock Haven, Pennsylvania). This is the oldest logbook, running from the aircraft's airworthiness certificate in 1974 through 2005, plus an AD compliance summary at the back. It predates and leads directly into [Airframe Log Book 2](airframe-log-2.md).

Where handwriting is faint or a stamped page overlaps other text, the transcription is best-effort and noted as such.
